Who I am:

My name is Raphael Gottfried Grubbauer, and I am a 14-year-old student living in the best country in the world - Austria.

I consider myself proficient in the following programming languages: C, C++, Python, Java, and JavaScript.

Additionally, I have experience with markup and data representation languages such as JSON, YAML, LaTeX, HTML, and CSS.

How It All started:

It all began when I was about six or seven years old. I watched a video about Minecraft featuring a random modpack. At one point, the YouTuber said something that stuck with me (translated from German): "[...] These mods were coded with Java, [...]"

That statement inspired me. I immediately wanted to learn Java - and I did, at least to some extent... However, I set it aside for a few years. Then, my middle school vice-principal handed me a brochure for a coding camp. I decided to attend, and for the past three years, I've been going back every year.

At the camp, I learned Python first, followed by C and C#. Later, I explored JavaScript and C++, which led me to create my first real project: MathOrDeath. To this day, I continue to develop and maintain it.
